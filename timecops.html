TimeCops

A simple, elegant stopwatch app for tracking multiple timers simultaneously.

Features

  • Multiple Stopwatches: Track as many timers as you need at once
  • Custom Names: Click any label to name your stopwatch
  • Easy Controls: Click the stopwatch body to start/stop timing
  • Precise Timing: Millisecond accuracy for all your timing needs
  • URL Templates: Your stopwatch setup is saved in the URL - bookmark it to create reusable templates

How to Use

  • Start/Stop: Click on the stopwatch circle to toggle timing
  • Name: Click on the label below the time to set a custom name
  • Add: Click "+ Add Stopwatch" to create more timers
  • Delete: Hover over a stopwatch and click the × button
  • Save Template: Name your stopwatches and bookmark the page - the URL contains your configuration

Template Examples

Create different setups for different needs:

  • Pomodoro: Set up timers for "Work", "Short Break", "Long Break"
  • Workout: Track different exercises or intervals
  • Cooking: Time multiple dishes simultaneously
  • Team Meetings: Track speaker time or agenda items
